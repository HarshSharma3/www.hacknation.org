About Us

Hacknation is a PAN India program focused on introducing students to the world of software development. Participants will work on multiple programming project with a community format. As a participant of Hacknation, students are assigned mentors from the community to gain exposure to real-world software development and techniques. Students have the opportunity to develop projects in areas related to their interest.

What is Hackathon?

A hackathon is an invention marathon. Programmers, designers, builders and more come together to learn, build, and share their creations throughout the weekend. Hackathons are not limited to computer science majors but for anyone who has an interest in technology and is eager to learn can participate in a hackathon. Not to be confused with illegal and unauthorized programming, "hacking" in this context means quickly and intelligently creating a real application that others can use.
